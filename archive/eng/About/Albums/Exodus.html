Summary

Zlabya, Jules, the rabbi and his cat travel to Paris for meeting Jules' family. But the rabbi never stops to criticize Paris sadness. He also always speaks about his nephew Raymond Rebibo who would become, in his mind, a great Parisian singer. It upsets Zlabya and Jules but amuses the rabbi's cat.
