Summary

Jules received books from Russia. But in the box there is also a russian painter. Nobody understand him except the rabbi's cat. This painter wants to go to Ethiopia in order to find Jerusalem of Africa. The rabbi, his cat, the painter and mister Vastenov the translator decide then to do an expedition for finding this place were Jews live in peace.
