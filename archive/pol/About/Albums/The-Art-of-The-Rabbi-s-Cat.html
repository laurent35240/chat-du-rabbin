The Art of The Rabbi's Cat

Drawer

 Joann Sfar

Publish date

 26.05.2011

Number of pages

 300

Editor

 Dargaud

Original title

 L'Art du Chat du Rabbin

Summary

While the Rabbi's Cat movie, written and directed by Joann Sfar, will soon be released, this book collects splendid research pictures drawn by some of the best current drawers, fantastic decors and colors from the movie itself. It helps to understand how a long animation movie is made. There is also a long interview with Sfar for enlightening the historical background where the story takes place: Jewish colonization, Arabs and France in Maghreb, etc. A very complete book about the movie and the story of the Rabbi's Cat.
