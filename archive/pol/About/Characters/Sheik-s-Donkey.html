Sheik's Donkey

The donkey is the companion of Sheik Mohammed Sfar.

In album Malka of the Lions, he is chatting with the cat in order to know if Sfar is a Jewish or Arabic name.

According to the Sheik, his donkey can read partitions and sing in three different language. Unfortunately he is singing out of tune.
