Father Lambert

Gallery

Father Lamber is mayor of Oran. He was elected to this place by promising Oran's people water.

His speech is extremist and racist:
"Is it that we want for future France? Jews and Arabs whose voice worths as much as ours? "
In front of this speech, Malka of Lions will slap him. Father Lambert will be surprised but will continue his talk.

This character really existed.
